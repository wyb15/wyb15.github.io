Yunbo WANG

I am an assistant professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I do research in deep learning, especially in the areas of predictive learning, model-based reinforcement learning, and intuitive physical inference.

I received my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Philip S. Yu and Prof. Mingsheng Long, and my B.S. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University. I also spent time in Prof. Joshua B. Tenenbaum's lab at MIT as a visiting researcher, working with Dr. Jiajun Wu.
